US News & World Report

User Research // SEO // Analyzing Data

Currently, I am the lead research analyst for the HomeGoods Category on the 360 Reviews Team at US News & World Report. I conduct and create the ratings for products, such as mattresses (toppers, cribs, air), vacuums, juicers, etc. We are currently working on the logic for our Mattress Recommendation Tool survey. The tool is to help us better understand our readers behaviors and lifestyle patterns. The user will be prompted with the survey once they land on the Best Mattresses page. The user data that we'll gather will provide us with more insight into what sleep problems do our readers currently face and what are they looking for when purchasing a mattresses. This will help provide information on what keywords and content to provide for future articles and rankings.
