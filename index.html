About

Hi, My name is Sloane Gartner. I am currently a student at James Madison Univeristy with a B.A in Interactive Design and Psychology. While applying for Madison's School of Media Arts and Design (SMAD), I had four concentrations to choose from, Creative Advertisement, Video, Journalism, and Interactive Design. Interactive design was my first concentration choice because I feel that the skills needed for designing interactive digital products are strong attributes to have in a world where the connection between users and products are growing. Also, I felt that having the Psychology background paired well, considering if I plan to make products for people I should know people. Through my interactive design classes, my interest grew in user experience and in learning how to design and integrate media elements.

Aside from acedemics, I am President of the Madison Club Field Hockey team. I work with other teams to schedule games and tournaments as well as traveling legistics. I hold meetings and create deadlines to ensure the executive board runs smoothly and effectivly. Also, since I have experience from being the previous Treasurer, I work on our yearly budget and organize the finances with the current tresurer.

When I'm not in class or on the field, I work at Madison's Technology and Design (TAD) as a staff photographer. TAD serves to complete creative projects for the departments of JMU University Unions. I take photos of events around campus, concerts, student run events, ceremonies, etc. I work with real-world clients and with deadlines that need to be met. Working at TAD has been one of my greatest experiences at Madison because it has given me the hands-on experience with project management that I need in order to be prepared for employment after graduation. I feel that the experiences I've had, have tought me to be a hard-worker, problem-solver, and dependable individual.
