About

My name is Sloane Gartner. I am currently a student at James Madison Univeristy with a B.A in Interactive Design and Psychology. While applying for Madison's School of Media Arts and Design (SMAD), I had four concentrations to choose from, Creative Advertisement, Video, Journalism, and Interactive Design. Interactive design was my first concentration choice because I feel that the skills needed for designing interactive digital products are strong attributes to have in a world where the connection between users and products are growing. Also, I felt that having the Psychology background paired well, considering if I plan to make products for people I should know people. Through my interactive design classes, my interest grew in user experience and in learning how to design and integrate media elements.
