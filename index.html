My name is Sloane Gartner. I am a research analyst for the 360 Reviews team at US News & World Report. I’ve been assisting with user research and developmenting methods and models to make recommendations about products and services to people who visit the 360 Reviews website. I've also been freelancing as a Web Designer & Photographer. I’ve worked with clients on their professional sites, logos, and overall design and layout of their website and web pages. I recently graduated from James Madison University with a B.A. in Interactive Design and Psychology and I would love to dive deeper into the world of design and UX/UI. Within my portfolio, you'll view my work as a designer and photographer, enjoy!.

I have a passion and interest in UX/UI design, web design, visual design, and front-end web development.
