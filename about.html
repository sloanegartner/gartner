Aside from academics, I am President of the James Madison Club Field Hockey team. I work with other teams to schedule games and tournaments as well as traveling legistics. I hold meetings and create deadlines to ensure the executive board runs smoothly and effectivly. Also, since I have experience from being the previous treasurer, I work on our yearly budget and organize the finances with the current treasurer.

When I'm not in class or on the field, I work as lead Photographer at James Madison's Technology and Design (TAD). TAD serves to complete creative projects for the departments of JMU's University Unions. I take photos of events around campus, concerts, student run events, ceremonies, etc. I work with real-world clients and with deadlines that need to be met. Working at TAD has been one of my greatest experiences at Madison because it has given me the hands-on experience with project management that I need in order to be prepared for employment after graduation. I feel that the experiences I've had, have tought me to be a hard-worker, problem-solver, and dependable individual.
