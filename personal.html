SHERPA

Web Design // Visual Layout // Organizing & Creating Content

SHERPA is a financial app that I designed for my B.A. in Interactive Design. I studied web and mobile interactions and design principles including usability, interactive design strategy, wire-framing, and web and mobile app prototype development. For this app, I conducted interviews, user and usability testing, and designed wireframes and prototypes. I created the SHERPA app in Figma. SHERPA is designed to help with money management. The app keep tracks of daily, weekly, and monthly payments. You can customize which topics of spending the app displays. The user can also add their cards to the app to track how much they spend on each card. This project is currently being worked on.

BarMe

SEO // Long Tail Keywords // Content Management

barMe is a mobile user interface that I am currently working on. The app is for those new to the scene, just turned 21, or you just want to find an amazing bar with great drinks and deals. barMe is your guide. Designed for young adults, the app allows for users to quickly find bars in their area as well as check wait times, invite friends, find drink specials, deals, reviews, and any events happening. The app is constantly updated, having the user effectivly check how packed the bar is minutes before arriving. barMe also grows and adapts with the more user interaction. The user is able to rate the bar they decide to visit. This rating is then averaged with other young adults in the area, creating a reliable rating. Never will you have to wait in the cold, be unsure about a drink, or walk into a bar blind, with barMe you will be prepared to have a good time. Drink responsibly and use barMe. The content and designs of the app were created through user research, feedback methodologies, wireframes, and prototypes.
