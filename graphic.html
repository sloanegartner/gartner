Branding Logo

Client's Personal Website // 2020

This logo was created for a client who wanted branding for his music website. The CR are the clients initals with the lines from sheet music in the backgorund. He plays piano and has piano lessons within the website, so I wanted to communicate that through his logo. I then made the CR a circle to symbolize the circle of fifths, which is the relationship among the 12 tones of the chromatic scale and is popular in music theory. Lastly, I decided on the typography to be similar to Rhapsody in Blue to play along with the piano and blue and gray theme of the site.

SHERPA is a finacial app I created in my mobile interaction design class. The apps function is to help users reach their goal of finacial independence. The general audience of the app were college students who were becoming overwhelmed with money management. The name SHERPA is to represent a Sherpa who helps travelers navigate the mountainous regions of Nepal and the Himalayas. They are renowned for their skill in mountaineering. The app acts as a Sherpa, in that it helps young adults navigate the rough terrain of money management. The logo is of mountains with a positive green arrow pointing up, symbolizing the growth in financial confidence with users.

The AleHouse

Interface Design and Web Delivery // 2020

The Alehouse is a website I created for my interactive design class. The website features breweries and wineries in the area of Harrisonburg, Virginia. Because the website features different types of local beers, the main symbol is a hop plant, which is a flowering plant often time used in beer. The plant's structure is to also take the form of a house. Since the beers, breweries, and wineries are local, I decided to make the logo a home to communicate a hometown feel.

Honey, I'm Home was a logo I created for businesses that produce their own honey. This was a project that was meant to teach me the features of Adobe Creative Cloud, and then use the tools to create a marketing campaign. The class focused on tools and techniques that are used to create graphic content for diverse media delivery systems. The design of the logo is suppose to be bug like in form, with bee stripes on the back, and honey comb wheels. They Slogan "Honey, I'm Home" refers to the home grown home that can be delivered to customers. I enjoyed this project because I interesting learning how to use Adobe Illustrator, along with the other Adobe features, to create something fun.
